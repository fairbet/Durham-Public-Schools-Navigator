The Durham Public Schools Navigator

Hack Duke 2016

By: Fred Xu, Matthew Barbano, and Ashka Stephens

This project assists Durham parents searching to find the optimal school for their children. It is based on grade rankings of Durham schools from the datasets North Carolina School Performance Data and Durham Public Schools Locations. Enter your address, the type of school (elementary, middle, or high), and minimum grade ranking (A, B, C, D, or F) that you would like to display. Click Show Top 3 Schools, and the map will do so. Make sure you enter a valid address and zoom out if no schools appear in your initial map view. Also, make sure your search criteria is not too restrictive, since the dataset we currently have is small (plans to enlarge soon).
